Know your obligations

Cloud contracts are complex. Whether you are a customer or reseller, the terms and conditions consist of intricate legal documents, and it can be hard to keep track of your vast responsibilities.

What can go wrong? Worst case, the provider switches off your access to the cloud.

Cloud Terms Guide helps you avoid this. The platform provides tools to assess, understand and negotiate cloud agreements from the key cloud service providers. The platform is regularly updated by legal experts and includes:

  • A risk assessment tool with concrete mitigating actions;
  • A risk register and proposed mitigations;
  • Tailor-made contract templates;
  • A practical guideline to maximise cloud benefits and mitigate business risks when utilizing cloud services from different cloud providers.
