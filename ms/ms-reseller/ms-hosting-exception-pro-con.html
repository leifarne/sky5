Summary, pro et con assessment of the various ways to contract as a SaaS supplier

The columns “EA”, “MCA” and “MOSA” shows the contractual risk, when you as a SaaS supplier use the “hosting exception” as your legal basis for using Azure services as part of your SaaS offering. E.g you are a customer of Microsoft yourself, under either the EA, MCA or Mosa program.

The column “you are a CSP” shows the contractual risk, when you as a SaaS supplier also act as a reseller for Microsoft, and the end-customer gets its own Microsoft subscription/tenant.

If we write N/A under an area, this means that there is no specific regulation.
