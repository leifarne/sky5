Detailed Walkthrough of MPA - Direct Bill

For a detailed walkthrough push the 'Risks' links.

Core Terms

The Core terms regulate general terms such as confidentiality, requirements for the processing of personal data in connection with resale, compliance obligations (related to law, HSE, ethical rules, anti-corruption rules, requirements for business conduct, training of the reseller's human resources, etc.), use of Microsoft's logo in connection with resale, term and termination, etc.

Risks

Channel Terms

These are terms that apply specifically to the reseller's resale of Microsoft Online Services. They must be combined with a "Channel Authorization". The terms include terms for resale and restrictions (you may not use Online Services internally, modify them, or provide warranties on Microsoft's behalf to the end customer beyond what Microsoft itself provides), general obligations, ordering rules, payment obligations, audit, warranty obligations and limitations of liability, third party claims, term and termination. Risks

Channel Terms

This is the document that actually authorize the reseller a right to resell Microsoft Online services. Here it is specified the territory in which you can resell, requirement to use the partner portal, requirement to ensure acceptance of the Microsoft terms and conditions by end customers, rules regarding suspension or cancellation of subscriptions, payment terms, requirement to provide support to end customers, etc. Risks

Guide to the Online Services and Software Channel Authorization

This document sets out additional requirements and guidelines for resale. For example, additional requirements for access to the MSMicrosoft Partner Portal (including requirements for multifactor authentication), additional requirements for end-customer support, product policies, other terms (such as customer satisfaction survey, invoice requirements), possibility to return/cancel orders, and also has a number of product-specific terms (for Azure services, Azure Stack, Skype, D365, rules for sale to public customers, etc.). Risks

Microsoft Partner Network Agreement

The agreement regulates the reseller's right to use the Microsoft Partner Network or related websites (the Partner Portal), including access to Microsoft marketing/sales materials, offers/campaigns, Microsoft products, etc. The Microsoft Partner Network Agreement does not change the Microsoft Partner Agreement, but it is a prerequisite for entering into and maintaining the Microsoft Partner Agreement.
